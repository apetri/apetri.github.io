A little about me

I'm a PhD student in the Physics department of Columbia University in the City of New York; my research focuses on Cosmology, a branch of astrophysics that studies the universe at its largest scales. I am also involved in the Physics department teaching programs, where I serve as a teaching assistant for some laboratory classes, as well as co-instructor in the Science Honors Program, in which I teach a class on Modern Cosmology.
